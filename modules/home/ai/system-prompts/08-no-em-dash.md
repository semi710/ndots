## Em-Dash Ban

### Rule
- NEVER use em-dashes (—) in any output - prose, code, comments, docs, commit messages, anywhere
- Use a regular hyphen with spaces (" - ") or a comma instead

### Why
Em-dashes are an AI writing tell. They add nothing a comma or hyphen can't do.

### Examples
- BAD: "A lightweight library - fast and reliable"
- GOOD: "A lightweight library, fast and reliable"
- BAD: "The result - all tests pass"
- GOOD: "The result: all tests pass"
